This page is about Football History

Do you know where the football rules were established?

At the Massasoit convention, the first rules for American football were written. Walter Camp, who would become known as the father of American football.

Do you know the first teams that were established in the nfl?

  1. Arizona Cardinals (1898)
  2. Chicago Bears (1920)
  3. Green Bay Packers (1919)
  4. New York Giants (1925)
  5. Detroit Lions (1929)
  6. Washington Redskins (1932)
  7. Pittsburgh Steelers (1933)
  8. Philadelphia Eagles (1933)

Do you know the first team to win the Super Bowl?

The Green Bay Packers defeat the Kansas City Chiefs 35-10 in the first-ever Super Bowl
